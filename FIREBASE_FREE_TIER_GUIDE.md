## ğŸ“‹ Step-by-Step: Setting Up Free Tier

### Step 1: Create Project (Free)
1. Go to https://console.firebase.google.com/
2. Click "Add project"
3. Enter project name
4. **Disable Google Analytics** (optional, but saves resources)
5. Click "Create project"
6. **No payment required!**

### Step 2: Enable Authentication (Free)
1. Go to **Authentication** â†’ **Get started**
2. Enable **Email/Password**
3. **Completely free!**

### Step 3: Create Firestore (Free)
1. Go to **Firestore Database**
2. Click **"Create database"**
3. Select **"Start in production mode"**
4. Choose location
5. Click **"Enable"**
6. **Free tier applies automatically!**

### Step 4: Enable Storage (Free)
1. Go to **Storage**
2. Click **"Get started"**
3. Click **"Next"** (default settings)
4. Choose location
5. Click **"Done"**
6. **Free tier applies automatically!**
